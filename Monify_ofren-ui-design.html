Why this palette. Near-monochrome and premium: a deep ink navy is the entire brand, on clean white surfaces with a whisper-cool page behind the cards. Colour is spent only where it means something — green for paid, amber for underpaid, rose for overdue — so the status of the money is the one thing that ever lights up. Boldness lives in the serif display numbers; everything else stays quiet and white.
